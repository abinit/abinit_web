Program and speakers

ABINIT Developer Workshop
May 31 - June 4, 2021

The workshop will take place entirely remotely. This format has been adopted due to the health situation associated with the COVID-19 pandemic.

The schedule has been set to be suitable for participants regardless of their connection point (Europe, US/CA, ...).
Real developer talks will be concentrated in the afternoon/evening (CET)!

  • Morning sessions: free or developer-user meetings
  • Afternoon sessions: scientific sessions
  • Evening sessions: discussions
