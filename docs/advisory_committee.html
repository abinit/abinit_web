International Advisory Committee

The role of the ABINIT International Advisory Committee (IAC) is to help make decisions concerning broad policies of ABINIT implementation, use, and licensing, and to help coordinate international activities of the ABINIT community. Examples of IAC activities include maintenance of the GPL licensing model within ABINIT, forging links to related coding projects, and coordinating ABINIT-sponsored Workshops and Schools.
