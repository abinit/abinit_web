Introduction

Initiated in 2002, the series of ABINIT developer workshops, organized each other year,
plays an important role in the life of the ABINIT community.
It is the occasion for the most active ABINIT developers, as well as a few expert users,
and selected invitees, to gather and exchange information, and present recent developments.
The future of ABINIT is also discussed, and recommendations are issued.

Plan

This year, the developer meeting will be held in Autrans.
All necessary information to get there are available on the location page.
The workshop will take place from Tuesday 24th March afternoon to Friday 27th March after lunch.

The program is available on-line with the abstracts of talks, announcements and posters.
