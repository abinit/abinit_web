ABI-Logix Consortium

ABI-Logix is a consortium of institutions which committed to provide logistic and technical support to the ABINIT software. Its role is to act as a functional interface between the Advisory Board, the Developers and the Users. As such it helps to coordinate the maintenance and development tasks of the software.

The partner institutions of the ABI-Logix consortium are:
