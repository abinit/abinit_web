2nd ABINIT Workshop

Paris, 10-12 May 2004

The aim of this workshop is to gather the most active members of the ABINIT developer group,
as well as a few expert users and selected invitees (outside the ABINIT project).
Intense exchange of information, about the latest and future developments, should strengthen the ABINIT project.
The future of ABINIT will also be discussed, and recommendations will be issued.


To favour interaction between active developers, invitees, and expert users, the number of attendees is limited to 40 .
The participation to this workshop is subject to invitation or approval by the organizers
(G. Zerah - F. Jollet - X. Gonze - G.-M. Rignanese ).
