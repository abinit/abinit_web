Registration

Registration closed!

 

Important note

Registration will be confirmed according to availability and access conditions in a European Super-computing Center. The number of participants is limited by the size of the hands-on rooms.
Thus the full registration procedure is going to be in two steps (this first form and an second one once you'll receive a registration acceptance confirmation)

 

Important information

The school is opened to students, postdocs and researchers.
The registration fee will cover lectures, accommodation and full board.
The fee depends on the choice of room you make (will be asked after acceptance of registration)  :

  • double room: 350€
  • single room: 450€
  • No accommodation (only lunches, breaks and social dinner): 100€
