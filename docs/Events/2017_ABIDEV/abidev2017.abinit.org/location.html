Location

Villa Clythia - CAES du CNRS (Holiday Village)
2754, rue Henri-Giraud
83600 Fréjus, France

 

Fréjus

Former military port and Roman city, Fréjus overlooks the rich alluvial plains that separate Estérel from the Massif des Maures.
Halfway between Cannes and St-Tropez, this city of art and culture has built up a rich historical and architectural heritage.
