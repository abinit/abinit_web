Registration

Registration closed!

 


Before you can apply for the school, you need to create an account (see Login Form on the left).

Afterwards, sign in and click on the "Registration" menu item again.
 

Note

The workshop is mainly on personal invitation. In any case, the number of participants will be limited to about 50 people.

The registration fee will cover accomodation, full board, lectures and evening in Saint-Raphaël.

The fee depends on the number of nights you spend in Fréjus:

  • 3 nights (from may 9 to may 12, 2017): 320€
  • 4 nights (from may 8 to may 12, 2017): 420€
